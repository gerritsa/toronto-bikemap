# Licenses And Attribution

The app must display attribution for:

- City of Toronto Open Data: `Contains information licensed under the Open Government Licence - Toronto.`
- OpenStreetMap: `© OpenStreetMap contributors`
- CARTO basemap: `© CARTO`
- Protomaps: `© Protomaps` only when a PMTiles/Protomaps basemap is published and used.

Library/data license notes:

- MapLibre GL JS: BSD-3-Clause.
- deck.gl: MIT.
- DuckDB/DuckDB WASM: MIT.
- OSRM backend: BSD-2-Clause.
- CARTO basemap tiles/styles: hosted third-party basemap; keep CARTO and OpenStreetMap attribution visible.
- Graph data from OpenStreetMap: ODbL, attribution required.
- Toronto Open Data: Open Government Licence - Toronto, attribution required and no endorsement implied.
- Protomaps: follow Protomaps attribution requirements if `manifest.assets.basemapUrl` points at a PMTiles basemap.
